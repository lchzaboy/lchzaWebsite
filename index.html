  • AMERICO

    a game!.

    So, I (Joel) was working on my 589th unpublished game and Jordan was planning on doing his 90980987th unfinished shortfilm, when we sat down and talked about making a commercial game for Americo with live-action cutscenes, so that we both get to do what we love to do, but actually taking our talent seriously and finish something that people would enjoy.

    Some prototypes have been already made, however I've been iterating over and over in that stage to make the controls of the game feel responsive and also trying to keep the graphics quality as high as possible while also keeping the framerate low.

    With that said, we expect to let you play some part of the game at the end of this year.
